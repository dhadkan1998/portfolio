About Me

Passionate about creating intelligent systems that solve real-world problems

Hello, I'm Dhadkan Shrestha

I am an AI/ML researcher with expertise in reinforcement learning, deep learning, and data visualization. My work focuses on developing intelligent systems that can operate autonomously in complex environments.

With a strong academic background and several peer-reviewed publications, I specialize in creating AI-powered solutions for robotics and autonomous systems. My research has applications in areas like emergency response, healthcare, and environmental monitoring.

I've developed systems for elderly fall detection, fire rescue robots, yoga pose estimation, and more using cutting-edge technologies like Docker, AWS, and Python. I'm certified in Google Data Analytics and Deep Learning Specializations.
